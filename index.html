Bay area transportation engineer excited about data, policy, design, and prosperity.

Hello! My name is Kate.

Riding my bicycle to work as a teenager cultivated a deep interest in transportation that was solidified as I came to understand transportation’s role in the environmental, social, and economic health of our communities. This understanding developed through diverse experiences with interdisciplinary research groups, government agencies, consulting firms, and advocacy organizations, culminating in my recent graduation from UC Berkeley’s transportation engineering graduate program. I am passionate about understanding the role that government plays in the well-being of all people and communicating what I learn with technical and non-technical folk alike.
